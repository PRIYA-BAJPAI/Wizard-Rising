ABOUT

Harry Potter is a globally famous seven-book fantasy series by J.K. Rowling, following orphaned Harry as he discovers he's a wizard, attends Hogwarts School, and battles the dark wizard Lord Voldemort, who killed his parents, all while navigating friendships with Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. The books chronicle Harry's life and magical education, becoming a massive pop culture phenomenon adapted into successful films and a continuing franchise.
